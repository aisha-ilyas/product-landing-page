Welcome, Bread Lovers!!!

Eve's Bakery was founded in the year 1999. It all began in a small shop in Ikeja, Lagos state, Nigeria, with 4 bakers and a manager. Now, there are at least 300 shops in Africa, over a 1000 franchises worldwide and over 10,000 employees.
